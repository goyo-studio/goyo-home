## Problem

Most office workers have tried to learn English. Few have made it a habit. The real problem isn't a lack of apps or content — it's that every session starts with a decision: what to study, which app to open, where to pick up. That friction kills consistency. After a few weeks, the routine breaks and never comes back.

## Description

English learning that works like a habit, not a task.
Every morning, today's lesson arrives on LINE — one curated video, key sentences, translations, and vocabulary. Nothing to decide, nothing to search. Open, learn for 5 minutes, done. The service removes every decision so the only thing left is showing up.

## Happy Case Senario

1. Add as a LINE friend.
2. Set your topic and level once.
3. Wake up to today's English — every morning, automatically.
