## Problem

People want a calm way to start or end their day, but meditation apps feel too abstract or too long, and contents are overwhelming and information-heavy. There's nothing that's short, quiet, and arrives every day — combining intellectual stimulation with emotional calm.

## Description

A daily ritual app that delivers one public domain masterpiece with a short description, AI-guided contemplation, a story or anecdote, and calming TTS audio.
No scrolling. No choosing. Just 5 minutes of quiet art, every day.

## Happy Case Senario

1. Open the app at your preferred time.
2. See today's artwork and listen.
3. Close and carry on with your day.
