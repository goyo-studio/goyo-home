## Problem

Most YouTube downloaders are packed with ads, dark patterns, and unnecessary features. They demand account information, install toolbars, and track user behavior  — just to save the videos. To download videos in the highest quality, you usually have to pay.

## Description

A minimal macOS utility that downloads YouTube videos locally.
No account. No tracking. No extra features.

## Happy Case Senario

1. Paste a link.
2. Choose quality.
3. Save a video.
